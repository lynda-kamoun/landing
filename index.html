Discover Japan by a student for students !

In 2022, during my 3rd year in my bachelor of Economics, I did an exchange at Meiji University in Tokyo and was able to meet a lot of students from different backgrounds but with one common denominator : we all almost missed our chance to go to Japan due to lack of information. This is why I came up with JapanMania to help future exchange students !
